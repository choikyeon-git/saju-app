Privacy Policy

1. General Provisions

Cho Ik Yeon (the "Developer") operates the Cheonmun mobile application (the "App"). The Developer values your personal information and complies with the Personal Information Protection Act. This Privacy Policy informs you how the personal information provided by users is processed and what measures are taken to protect it.

2. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

The App processes personal information for the following purposes:

  • Provision of fortune-telling services: Saju analysis, horoscope analysis, and related content.
  • Disclaimer: The contents provided in the App are for entertainment and reference purposes only. Users are solely responsible for any major or minor choices, including investments.

3. Items of Personal Information Processed

The App processes the following information entered directly by the user for fortune analysis. This data is processed locally on the user's device and is not transmitted to an external server.

  • Required items: Date of birth, time of birth, gender, pen name.
  • Optional items: Name (nicknames or aliases are acceptable).

4. Period of Processing and Retention of Personal Information

The App does not collect, store, or share the user's personal information on any separate server. The entered information is used only temporarily within the user's device to calculate results, and all information is immediately destroyed when the app is closed or the screen is refreshed.

5. Rights and Obligations of the Data Subject

Users may refuse to enter personal information at any time, in which case the use of some functions of the app may be restricted. Since this App does not store information on a server, a separate request for deletion is not required.
